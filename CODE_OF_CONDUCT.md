# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the BMad Marketplace a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Submitting modules with deceptive, malicious, or harmful intent

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions within the BMad Marketplace — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any communication related to module submissions and reviews.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a GitHub issue or contacting the BMad team directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. The BMad team reserves the right to remove modules, reject submissions, or ban contributors who violate this Code of Conduct.
